// Work and School

I’m a senior Computer Science student at Northern Arizona University, and expect to graduate in May 2021. Over the past 2 years, I’ve been a Teaching Assistant (TA) for Data Structures and Algorithms (CS 249) and Automata Theory (CS 315). I honed my recursive thinking during 2 semesters as a Data Structures and Algorithms TA, and I'm continually challenged to think differently about computation when helping students with Automata Theory. I’ve also been a Research Assistant in 2 different labs. The first lab I worked in was the NAU IoT Research Lab, where I led development on an overhead occupancy counter that employed open-source libraries and a Raspberry Pi. I now work in a different lab which is funded by a partnership between NAU and the Air Force Research Lab (AFRL). Here, I actively work on developing libraries for use in the simulation of a secure processor that runs on a RISC-V architecture. I’ve also been a Research Assistant in 2 different labs.
